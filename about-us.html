ABOUT US

WELCOME TO THE

DORO FOUNDATION

Doro is derived from Ancient Greek meaning "gift".


We believe major life insurance donations may provide one of the most significant gifts an individual can make. Donating a life insurance policy and continuing to pay premiums is not a feasible option for most people and the primary reason policies are cancelled is because premiums increase or become a burden. However, there is often tremendous value hidden inside life insurance policies far above any potential Cash Surrender Value. Even term policies with no cash value, through our process, can provide significant donations.


The Doro Foundation exists specifically to streamline the donation & liquidation of life insurance policy assets and utilizes LS Hub as its administrative partner. LS Hub provides managerial, gift consulting, administration, technology and operational functions for our Life Insurance Major Gift program. To learn more about LS Hub, visit www.LSHub.net.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to provide a solution for non-profits & philanthropic individuals to maximize the value of life insurance major gifts and effectuate donations by providing a custom tailored solution based on trust & transparency.

WHO WE ARE

The Doro Foundation is a national nonprofit that was created to manage the process of receiving, servicing, liquidating and lastly granting funds from life insurance donations.

WHAT WE DO

We enable other nonprofits to outsource the expertise needed and potential risks of accepting donations of life insurance. Our team is highly experienced in this process and ensures the best experience for all.

WHY WE DO IT

Each year policies that could be sold for over $30 billion ($180 billion of face amount) are lapsed. This is an enormous philanthropic opportunity we support and are passionate about doing the right way.
