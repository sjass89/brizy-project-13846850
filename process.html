Step 2

Complete Donation Paperwork

If there is a donation opportunity we will provide paperwork for the donor and insured to complete that provides:

  • Authorizations to determine Fair Market Value
  • Agreements to facilitate the donation properly


*The donor can review the FMV and decide to donate all, none, or an unrestricted percentage of the policy.

Step 3

Transfer Policy & Qualified Appraisal

Once the donor has decided how they would like to proceed the final transfer paperwork is completed. Ownership is then transfered* and a qualified appraisal obtained.

*At least 2 months of premium must be in the policy to ensure the process can be completed.

Step 4

Liquidate Policy & Grant Proceeds

The Doro Foundation's team is highly experienced in the process of liquidating life insurance policies and will effectuate the process as quickly as possible.

Once the asset has been liquidated the donor can recommend a non-profit to receive the proceeds and upon due diligence and approval the entire net proceeds are transferred.

WHO WE ARE

The Doro Foundation is a national nonprofit that was created to manage the process of receiving, servicing, liquidating and lastly granting funds from life insurance donations.

WHAT WE DO

We enable other nonprofits to outsource the expertise needed and potential risks of accepting donations of life insurance. Our team is highly experienced in this process and ensures the best experience for all.

WHY WE DO IT

Each year policies that could be sold for over $30 billion ($180 billion of face amount) are lapsed. This is an enormous philanthropic opportunity we support and are passionate about doing the right way.
