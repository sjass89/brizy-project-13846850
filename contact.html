WHO WE ARE

The Doro Foundation is a national nonprofit that was created to manage the process of receiving, servicing, liquidating and lastly granting funds from life insurance donations.

WHAT WE DO

We enable other nonprofits to outsource the expertise needed and potential risks of accepting donations of life insurance. Our team is highly experienced in this process and ensures the best experience for all.

WHY WE DO IT

Each year policies that could be sold for over $30 billion ($180 billion of face amount) are lapsed. This is an enormous philanthropic opportunity we support and are passionate about doing the right way.
